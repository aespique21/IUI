Filipino

I was born in the Philippines and lived there for 4 years before I came to America. Indiana was were I was raised from that day on and we haven't lived anywhere else since.


Favorite Food

I love all Filipino food, but my favorite has to be pancit malabon, which is a type of noodle that is savory and salty, but the saltiness depends on how you like it. My mom makes it just right. A good amount of savory and salty. Shanghai is also a favorite for me, which is a type of egg roll made with ground pork and some finely chopped vegetables.
